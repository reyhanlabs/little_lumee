Listen & Learn
Dengarkan percakapan berikut dengan teliti
Percakapan 1 — Menggambar Bentuk
Sari dan Doni menggambar bentuk dan warna bersama
👧
Sari
Look! I drew a red circle.
🧒
Doni
Nice! I drew a blue square.
👧
Sari
What is your favorite shape?
🧒
Doni
My favorite shape is a yellow star!
Percakapan 2 — Pelangi
Guru menjelaskan warna-warna pelangi kepada murid
👨‍🏫
Teacher
Look outside! There is a rainbow. What colors do you see?
👧
Lina
I see red, orange, and yellow!
👨‍🏫
Teacher
What else can you see?
👧
Lina
I see green, blue, and purple too!
📌

Pola "a/an + warna + bentuk" sangat umum digunakan. Contoh: "a red circle", "a blue square", "an orange star".
