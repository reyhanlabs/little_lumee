📖 Reading · Chapter 5

My School

Belajar membaca teks tentang sekolah menggunakan pola "This is my..." dan "My... is..."

⏱ ~20 menit
📝 8 soal latihan
📄 2 teks bacaan
🔣
Pola Kalimat — My School
Pahami dulu sebelum membaca
Pola 1 — Menunjukkan Milik Sendiri
This is + my [benda/orang]
📌 This is my school. · This is my teacher.
Pola 2 — Mendeskripsikan dengan Kata Sifat
My [benda/orang] + is + [sifat]
📌 My teacher is kind. · My classroom is big.
💡

Kata "my" dipakai untuk menyebut sesuatu yang menjadi milik kita sendiri, seperti "my book" atau "my desk".

📖
Baca & Pahami
Baca kedua teks berikut dengan teliti
📄 Teks 1 — My School

My name is Mia. This is my school. I go to school every day with my friends.

My teacher is kind. Her name is Miss Sarah. My classroom is big and bright.

There are twenty students in my class. I love my school very much!

📄 Teks 2 — In My Classroom

This is my desk. I sit here every day. This is my book, and this is my pencil.

My pencil is yellow. I use it to write on my book and look at the blackboard.

I am a happy student. I like learning new things every day.

📌

Kata hijau = pola kalimat penting. Kata ungu = vocabulary sekolah. Baca berulang untuk menghafal!

3. What color is the pencil in Text 2?
4. What does the writer use the pencil for in Text 2?
