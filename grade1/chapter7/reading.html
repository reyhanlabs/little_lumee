Food & Drinks

Baca dan pahami teks tentang makanan favorit dan kebiasaan makan sehari-hari.

⏱ ~20 menit
📝 8 soal latihan
📚 2 teks bacaan
📖
Pola Kalimat
Pelajari cara mengungkapkan makanan favorit dalam bahasa Inggris
Pola 1: Menyatakan Makanan Favorit
I like + (noun)
Contoh: "I like rice."
Artinya: "Saya suka nasi."
Pola 2: Makanan yang Dimakan Sehari-hari
I eat + (noun) + for + (waktu)
Contoh: "I eat bread for breakfast."
Artinya: "Saya makan roti untuk sarapan."
📚
Bacaan
Baca kedua teks dan perhatikan kosakata yang di-highlight
Passage 1: My Favorite Meal
Hello! My name is Siti. I like food very much. My favorite meal is breakfast. I eat bread and eggs every morning. I also drink milk. Sometimes I eat an apple too. All of these foods are healthy and delicious. What is your favorite food?
Passage 2: Healthy Foods
Ali and his mother go to the market. They buy chicken, rice, and fresh vegetables. At home, his mother cooks chicken and rice for lunch. They also have water to drink. Ali's favorite food is noodles, but he eats healthy food every day. Good nutrition is important for our health!
PART A
Pilihan Ganda (Multiple Choice)
Pilih jawaban yang paling benar — 4 soal
1. Apa makanan favorit Siti menurut Passage 1?
2. Apa yang Siti minum setiap pagi?
3. Siapa yang memasak makanan sehat di Passage 2?
4. Apa yang dibeli Ali dan ibunya di pasar?
