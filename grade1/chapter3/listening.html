Listen & Learn
Dengarkan percakapan berikut dengan teliti
Percakapan 1 — Menunjukkan Foto Keluarga
Lisa menunjukkan foto keluarganya kepada teman
👧
Lisa
Look! This is my family. This is my father. This is my mother.
🧒
Andi
Wow! Who is this boy?
👧
Lisa
He is my brother. His name is Rio.
🧒
Andi
And who is this girl?
👧
Lisa
She is my sister. Her name is Dewi.
Percakapan 2 — Mengunjungi Kakek dan Nenek
Budi bercerita tentang kunjungannya ke rumah kakek
🧒
Budi
Yesterday, I visited my grandparents. This is my grandfather. He is very kind.
👧
Sari
That's nice! What about your grandmother?
🧒
Budi
She is my grandmother. She makes delicious food!
👧
Sari
Do you have any baby siblings?
🧒
Budi
Yes! I have a baby sister. This is my baby sister, Nina!
📌

Gunakan "He" untuk laki-laki dan "She" untuk perempuan. Contoh: "He is my brother" dan "She is my sister".
