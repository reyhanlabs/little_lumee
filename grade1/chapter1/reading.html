📖 Reading · Chapter 1

Hello, World!

Belajar membaca teks perkenalan dan memahami pola kalimat dasar bahasa Inggris.

⏱ ~20 menit
📝 8 soal latihan
📄 2 teks bacaan
🔣
Pola Kalimat Perkenalan
Pahami strukturnya sebelum membaca
Pola 1 — Menyebutkan Nama
My name + is + [nama kamu]
📌 Contoh: My name is Sari. · My name is Doni.
Pola 2 — Menyebutkan Identitas
I am + [usia / perasaan]
📌 Contoh: I am seven years old. · I am happy.
💡

"My name is" dan "I am" adalah dua pola paling sering digunakan saat perkenalan. Hafal keduanya!

📖
Baca & Pahami
Baca teks berikut dengan teliti
📄 Teks 1 — My Name is Rara
Hello! My name is Rara. I am six years old. I am a student. My school is SDN Matahari.

I have a best friend. Her name is Mia. Mia is also six years old. We are happy friends.

Nice to meet you! My favorite color is pink. I like to read books. Do you like to read too?
📄 Teks 2 — Hello, I am Adi!
Hi! My name is Adi. I am seven years old. I live in Bekasi.

I have a brother. His name is Bayu. Bayu is nine years old. We go to school together every day.

My favorite animal is a cat. My cat's name is Mimi. Nice to meet you! What is your name?
📌

Kata yang berwarna hijau adalah ekspresi penting. Kata yang berwarna ungu adalah kata kunci atau nama. Baca berulang sampai kamu paham!
