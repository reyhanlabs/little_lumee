Listen & Learn
Dengarkan percakapan berikut dengan seksama
Percakapan 1 — Saling Menyapa
Dengarkan bagaimana Lily dan Tom saling menyapa
👧
Lily
Hello! My name is Lily. What is your name?
👦
Tom
Hi! My name is Tom. Nice to meet you, Lily!
👧
Lily
Nice to meet you too, Tom! How are you?
👦
Tom
I am fine, thank you! And you?
👧
Lily
I am fine too. Thank you!
📌

Perhatikan kata-kata yang ditebalkan — itu adalah vocabulary penting yang harus kamu ingat!

Percakapan 2 — Di Kelas Baru
Dengarkan bagaimana Budi memperkenalkan diri di kelas
👨‍🏫
Teacher
Hello! Class, we have a new friend today. Please introduce yourself!
🧒
Budi
Hello, everyone! My name is Budi. I am seven years old. Nice to meet you all!
👨‍🏫
Teacher
Nice to meet you too, Budi! How are you today?
🧒
Budi
I am very happy, thank you!
