Dialog 1: My Daily Routine
Cerita tentang rutinitas harian seorang anak
Dialog 1 Transcript:
👦
Ravi
"Good morning! I wake up at seven o'clock. I eat breakfast. Then I go to school."
👦
Ravi
"At school, I study English and Math. After school, I play with my friends."
👦
Ravi
"In the evening, I watch TV and do my homework. Before I go to bed, I drink milk. Good night!"

3. Jam berapa Siti pergi ke sekolah di dialog 2?
4. Apa yang dilakukan Siti sebelum tidur?
