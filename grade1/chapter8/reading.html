📖 Reading · Chapter 8

My Day

Baca dan pahami teks tentang rutinitas dan kegiatan sehari-hari.

⏱ ~20 menit
📝 8 soal latihan
📚 2 teks bacaan
📖
Pola Kalimat
Pelajari cara menceritakan aktivitas sehari-hari
Pola 1: Aktivitas Harian dengan Waktu
I + (verb) + at + (time)
Contoh: "I wake up at seven o'clock."
Artinya: "Saya bangun jam tujuh pagi."
Pola 2: Urutan Aktivitas Sehari-hari
First... Then... After... Finally...
Contoh: "First I wake up. Then I eat breakfast. Finally I go to school."
Artinya: "Pertama saya bangun. Kemudian saya makan. Akhirnya saya pergi ke sekolah."
📚
Bacaan
Baca kedua teks dan perhatikan kosakata yang di-highlight
Passage 1: Ali's Day
My name is Ali. I have a busy day every day. In the morning, I wake up at 6:30. I eat breakfast with my family. Then I go to school at 7:30. At school, I study English, Math, and Science. I also play with my friends during break time. After school, I watch TV for one hour. Before I go to bed, I do my homework. I always sleep at 9:00 PM. My day is very interesting!
Passage 2: Siti's Routine
Hello! I am Siti. Every day is important for me. First, I wake up early at 6:00 AM. Then I eat a healthy breakfast with eggs and bread. My mother and I go to school together. I am very diligent and I study hard in every class. Sometimes I play badminton after school. In the evening, I watch TV a little bit and then I go to bed early. I always sleep at 8:30 PM because I need rest for tomorrow. This is my daily schedule!
PART A
Pilihan Ganda (Multiple Choice)
Pilih jawaban yang paling benar — 4 soal
1. Jam berapa Ali bangun di pagi hari?
2. Apa yang dilakukan Siti sebelum tidur?
3. Jam berapa Siti tidur?
4. Apa yang dilakukan Ali untuk bersantai setelah sekolah?
Part B
Isian — Lengkapi Kalimat
Isi dengan kata dari passage — 4 soal
5.
Ali at 6:30 AM.
6.
Siti a healthy breakfast.
7.
Both Ali and Siti every day.
8.
Ali at 9:00 PM.
