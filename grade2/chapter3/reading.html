📖 Reading

Clothes & Appearance

Read and understand passages about what people wear and how they look.

📝
Grammar Patterns
Pola kalimat penting yang perlu diingat
"I am wearing a [clothing]." Example: I am wearing a shirt.
"My [body part] is [color/adjective]." Example: My hair is black.
"[Person] is wearing [clothing]." Example: Ali is wearing pants.
"[Person] has [adjective] [appearance]." Example: Rina has a pretty smile.
💡

Tips: Identifikasi subject-verb-object dalam setiap kalimat. Coba buat kalimat sendiri dengan pola ini. Grammar sangat penting!

📚
Reading Passages
Teks tentang fashion dan penampilan
Passage 1: What I'm Wearing Today
Hello! My name is Ali. Today I am wearing a blue shirt and black pants. My shoes are white. I also have a jacket because it is cold. My hat is gray and my socks are blue. I like to dress nicely every day!
Passage 2: My Family Looks Like
My family is very different. My dad is tall and handsome. He has short black hair. My mom has long brown hair and big brown eyes. She has a pretty smile. My brother is short and has curly hair. My sister is tall like my dad. We all look different but we are happy together!
📌

Saat membaca, fokus pada kata-kata yang di-highlight. Ini adalah vocabulary kunci tentang pakaian dan penampilan!
