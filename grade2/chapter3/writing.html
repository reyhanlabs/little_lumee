✏️
Guided Writing Prompts
Panduan menulis kalimat yang benar
Prompt 1: What Am I Wearing Today?
Describe the clothes you are wearing today. Start with "Today I am wearing..." Use at least 3-4 clothing items and describe their colors.
Prompt 2: Describe a Friend's Appearance
Describe how a friend looks. Include their hair color, eye color, whether they are tall or short, and any other features you like about them.
Prompt 3: My Favorite Clothes
Write about your favorite piece of clothing. Explain what it looks like, what color it is, and why you like wearing it.
📌
