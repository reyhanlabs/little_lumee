Listen & Learn
Dengarkan percakapan dan pelajari konteksnya
Dialog 1: My New Clothes
Ali menunjukkan pakaian barunya dan mendiskusikan gaya fashion
👦
Ali
I have new clothes! I am wearing a blue shirt and black pants.
🧒
Friend
That looks nice! What about your shoes?
👦
Ali
I am wearing white shoes. I also have a new hat and a jacket.
Dialog 2: Physical Appearance
Siti mendeskripsikan penampilan fisik temannya dengan adjective yang tepat
👧
Siti
My friend Rina has long black hair and big brown eyes. She has a pretty smile.
🧒
Friend
Is she tall or short?
👧
Siti
She is tall and handsome. Everyone likes her friendly face.
📌

Dengarkan dengan fokus pada detail pakaian dan deskripsi yang disebutkan dalam percakapan!

PART A
Multiple Choice Questions
Pilih jawaban yang tepat — 6 soal
1. What color is Ali's shirt?
2. What color are Ali's pants?
3. What color are Ali's shoes?
4. What color is Rina's hair?
5. What color are Rina's eyes?
6. Is Rina tall or short?
PART B
Fill in the Blanks
