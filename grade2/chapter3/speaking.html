Practice Sentences
Ucapkan kalimat tentang pakaian
1. I am wearing a blue shirt.
2. My pants are black and my shoes are white.
3. I have long black hair and brown eyes.
4. My friend is tall and handsome.
5. She has a pretty smile and a friendly face.
📌

Dengarkan cara pengucapan yang benar untuk setiap nama pakaian. Coba beberapa kali sampai terbiasa!

Multiple Choice Questions

1. What is Ali wearing?
2. What color are Ali's pants?
3. What color are Ali's shoes?
4. What color is Rina's hair?
5. Is Rina tall or short?
6. What is Rina's smile like?
