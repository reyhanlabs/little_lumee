🗣️
Practice Sentences
Dengarkan dan coba ucapkan kalimat berikut
1. This is my house.
2. My bedroom is big and comfortable.
3. The living room has a soft sofa.
4. The kitchen is next to the dining room.
5. I can see the garden from my window.
💡

Tips: Dengarkan, pause, lalu ucapkan dengan intonasi yang sama. Rekam voice kamu dan bandingkan dengan native speaker untuk memperbaiki pronunciation!

PART A
Multiple Choice / Pilihan Ganda
Jawab berdasarkan pemahaman kalimat
1. "My bedroom is big and..." What comes next?
2. What furniture is in the living room?
3. Where is the kitchen?
4. What can be seen from the window?
5. How many sentences to practice?
6. Sentence 2 describes...
