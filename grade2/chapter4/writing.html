House & Rooms

🎯
Writing Guide
Panduan menulis yang benar untuk soal essay
1. Gunakan pola kalimat yang sudah dipelajari: "This is my...", "My ... has a...", "The ... is ...".
2. Tulis minimal 2 kalimat lengkap dengan huruf kapital di awal dan titik di akhir.
3. Gunakan adjective (kata sifat) untuk mendeskripsikan room dan furniture: big, small, comfortable, clean.
💡

Tips: Buat kalimat tentang rumahmu atau kamarmu. Bayangkan kamu sedang mengajari teman tentang tempat tinggalmu!

PART A
Essay / Esai Bebas
Tulis tentang rumahmu atau kamarmu
📝 PROMPT 1: ABOUT MY HOUSE
Write about your house. Describe what rooms it has and what furniture is in each room. Use at least 3 sentences.
📝 PROMPT 2: MY FAVORITE ROOM
Write about your favorite room in your house. Describe the room and explain why you like it. Use at least 2-3 sentences.
💡

Tips: Jangan khawatir dengan kesalahan kecil. Yang penting adalah kamu bisa menulis kalimat lengkap dengan pola yang benar. Baca kembali sebelum submit!

PART B
Multiple Choice / Pilihan Ganda
Soal pilihan ganda tentang writing
1. Which sentence structure is correct?
2. Complete: "The kitchen is..." (choose adjective)
3. What is an article?
4. Which sentence has correct capitalization?
5. "The living room has a soft..." (choose noun)
6. How many sentences minimum in PROMPT 1?
