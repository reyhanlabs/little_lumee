📖 Reading

House & Rooms

Baca teks tentang rumah dan kamar untuk memahami kosakata dan ungkapan bahasa Inggris.

📝
Grammar Patterns
Pola kalimat yang sering muncul
"This is my [room]." Contoh: This is my bedroom.
"My [room] has a [furniture]." Contoh: My bedroom has a bed.
"The [furniture] is [adjective]." Contoh: The sofa is comfortable.
"[Room] is located next to the [room]." Contoh: The kitchen is located next to the dining room.
💡

Tips: Kata yang biru bold = struktur kalimat penting. Coba buat kalimatmu sendiri dengan pola ini!

📚
Read & Understand
Baca kedua teks berikut dengan teliti
📄 Text 1 — Our House Layout

Welcome to our house! The living room is very big and comfortable. There is a soft sofa and a large table in the living room. Next to the living room is the kitchen. The kitchen is clean and modern. The dining room is next to the kitchen. We have a big table in the dining room for family meals. Upstairs, there are three bedrooms. The hallway connects all the rooms. We also have a bathroom on each floor. Our house is our happy home!

📄 Text 2 — My Bedroom is Cozy

My bedroom is my favorite room in the house. It is small but very comfortable. I have a soft bed where I sleep every night. Next to my bed is a small desk where I do my homework. My room has a big window. From the window, I can see the beautiful garden with green plants and flowers. The walls are painted light blue, which makes the room feel calm and peaceful. I have a clean floor and like to keep everything neat. My bedroom is my own special place where I can rest and study.

💡

Tips: Kata yang biru = frasa penting yang perlu diingat. Underline atau tandai kata-kata sulit, lalu coba pahami artinya dari konteks.

PART A
Multiple Choice / Pilihan Ganda
Pilih jawaban berdasarkan teks yang telah dibaca
1. What is the living room like?
2. What room is next to the living room?
3. Where is the dining room located?
4. What color is the bedroom painted?
5. What can be seen from the bedroom window?
6. How many bedrooms does the house have?
