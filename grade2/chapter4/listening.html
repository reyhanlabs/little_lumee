Listen & Learn
Dengarkan percakapan berikut dengan teliti
Percakapan 1 — Welcome to My House
Ali: "Welcome to my house! This is the living room."
Friend: "It's very big and comfortable! What's that door?"
Ali: "That's the kitchen. And next to it is the dining room. My bedroom is upstairs."
Friend: "Your house is beautiful!"
Percakapan 2 — My Favorite Room
Siti: "My favorite room is my bedroom. It's small but very cozy."
Friend: "What furniture do you have?"
Siti: "I have a comfortable bed, a desk for homework, and a big window. I can see the garden from my window."
💡

Tips: Kata yang di-bold ungu = vocabulary dan frasa penting tentang rumah & kamar. Dengarkan berkali-kali sampai kamu bisa memahami tanpa membaca!

PART A
Multiple Choice / Pilihan Ganda
Pilih jawaban yang paling benar berdasarkan percakapan
1. What is the first room Ali shows his friend?
2. How does the friend describe the living room?
3. Where is Ali's bedroom?
4. What kind of room is Siti's bedroom?
5. What furniture does Siti have in her bedroom?
6. What can Siti see from her window?
