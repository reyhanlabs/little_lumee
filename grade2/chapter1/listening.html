Listen & Learn
Follow the dialogues and count along
Dialog 1: Ali's Fruits
Ali: "Look! I have two apples and three oranges."
Friend: "How many fruits do you have altogether?"
Ali: "Two plus three is five!"
Dialog 2: Siti's Favorite Number
Siti: "My favorite number is seven. It is lucky!"
Friend: "Why do you like the number seven?"
Siti: "Because it is lucky. I also have ten books!"
