Guided Writing Prompts
Write sentences about each topic
Prompt 1: My Family
Describe your family. What are their names? How many family members do you have? What do they like?
Prompt 2: My Pet
Tell us about a pet you have or want. What is its name? What color is it? How is it?
Prompt 3: Family & Pets Together
Write about your family and pets. Do they play together? What activities do you do together?
