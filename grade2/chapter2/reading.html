📖 Reading

Family & Pets

Read and understand passages about family members and pets.

📝
Grammar Patterns
Common sentence structures in this chapter
"I have a [pet]." Example: I have a dog.
"My [family member] is [adjective]." Example: My mom is kind.
"This is my [person/pet]." Example: This is my brother.
"[Person] has [number] [pets]." Example: Ali has two brothers.
📚
Reading Passages
Read the passages carefully
Passage 1: My Happy Family
My name is Ali. I have a big family. My mom is kind and my dad is strong. I have two brothers and one sister. We live together in a nice house. My brothers like to play football, and my sister likes to read books. We are happy together.
Passage 2: Pets at Home
Hello! My name is Siti. I have a cute dog. His name is Max. Max is brown and white. He is very friendly and likes to play. My brother also has a cat. The cat is black. We love our pets very much!
